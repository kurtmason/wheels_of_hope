Our Mission

Building Community, One Bike at a Time

We believe that every child deserves access to transportation, exercise, and the simple joy that comes from riding a bicycle.

Wheels of Hope is a community-driven foundation dedicated to providing bicycles to children who need them most. Founded on the principle that mobility is freedom, we work to ensure no child is left behind.

Through generous donations and community nominations, we've connected hundreds of children with bikes that provide not just transportation, but confidence, independence, and joy. Every bicycle we distribute is carefully inspected, refurbished, and fitted to ensure safety and longevity.

We rely on our community to help identify children who would benefit from receiving a bicycle. Whether it's a family facing financial hardship, a child who has overcome challenges, or someone who simply deserves a bright spot in their lifeâ€”we want to hear about them.

How It Works

Three Simple Steps to Make a Difference

1. Nominate

Know a child who could use a bicycle? Submit a nomination through our form. Tell us about the child and why they deserve this gift.

2. Review

Our team reviews all nominations with care and consideration. We work directly with families and community partners to verify needs.

3. Deliver

Once approved, we match the child with a refurbished bike, helmet, and lock. Delivery is coordinated to create a memorable moment.

Donate

Have a bike to donate? We accept bicycles in any condition, along with helmets, locks, and accessories.

Refurbish

Our volunteer mechanics inspect and repair all donated bikes to ensure they're safe and ready to ride.

Impact

Every bike distributed creates ripples of joy in our community. Recipients often pay it forward as they grow.
